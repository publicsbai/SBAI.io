Research

Our research focuses on the intricate interplay between aging and the immune system. Using a systems biology approach, we integrate multi-omics data to understand the molecular mechanisms underlying immunosenescence and age-related diseases. Our goal is to identify novel biomarkers and therapeutic targets to promote healthy aging.
